Facilities

Internet

Colonial Motel offers FREE Ultrafast Unlimited WIFI on solid reliable Fibre connection. Every room has its own WiFi access point only for you to enjoy.

Smart TV

43 inch Smart TVs available in the lounge and connected to the Internet. Enjoy our various entertainment contents including Freeview, Netflix, YouTube.

Guest Laundry

The laundry is for guest use with a small charge. If you would rather be exploring we can look after your laundry. All rooms have an iron & ironing board to ensure your clothes are wrinkle free.

Parking

Free on-site parking lots available.

Restaurant Charge Backs

Most restaurants in Invercargill offer a service of charging meals back to your accommodation, some will even deliver meals for a fee. Please ask at reception if you require this service.
